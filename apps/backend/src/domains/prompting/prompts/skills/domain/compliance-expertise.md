## Compliance Domain Knowledge

**HOS Rules:** 11-hour driving limit, 14-hour on-duty window, 70-hour 8-day cycle, 34-hour restart. 30-minute break required after 8 hours of driving. Short-haul exception: 150 air-mile radius, 14-hour duty period, no ELD required.

**Required Documents by Load Type:** Dry van (BOL, rate con, POD). Reefer (+ temp logs, pulp test). Flatbed (+ securement photos, oversize permits if >8.5ft wide). Hazmat (+ placard verification, shipping papers, emergency response info). Oversized (+ route survey, pilot car requirements, state permits).

**CDL/Medical/Insurance Renewal Windows:** CDL renewal varies by state (4-8 years). Medical certificate every 2 years (annually for certain conditions). Auto liability insurance renewal annually. Cargo insurance renewal annually.

**ELD Mandate:** All CMVs in interstate commerce must use ELDs. Exceptions: pre-2000 engines, short-haul, driveaway-towaway. Supporting documents retention: 6 months.

**Drug Testing:** Pre-employment, random (50% pool for controlled substances, 10% for alcohol), post-accident (if criteria met), reasonable suspicion, return-to-duty, follow-up. FMCSA Clearinghouse: query required before hiring AND annually for all current drivers (49 CFR 382.701). MVR must be reviewed annually per 49 CFR 391.25.

**HOS Exceptions:** Adverse driving conditions exception: 2 extra hours of driving allowed when conditions (weather, road closures) not known before dispatch. Short-haul exception: 150 air-mile radius, 14-hour window, no ELD required, time card instead.
