## Safety Risk Management

**CSA BASIC Categories:** Unsafe Driving (speeding, reckless, improper lane change). HOS Compliance (logbook violations, driving beyond limits). Vehicle Maintenance (brake, light, tire defects). Controlled Substances (positive drug/alcohol tests). Hazmat Compliance (loading, placarding). Crash Indicator (crash involvement rate). Driver Fitness (licensing, medical).

**Score Thresholds:** Unsafe Driving >65% = FMCSA intervention. HOS >65% = investigation. Vehicle Maintenance >80% = focused inspection. Controlled Substances >80% = investigation.

**Insurance Coverage (49 CFR 387.9):** Auto liability minimums: general freight $750K, hazmat (non-bulk) $1M, hazmat (bulk/poison/explosives) $5M, household goods $300K-$5K per vehicle. Cargo insurance: $100K minimum (varies by commodity). General liability. Workers comp (for W-2 employees). Occupational accident (for 1099 contractors).

**Claims Process:** First notice of loss → adjuster assignment → documentation gathering → liability determination → subrogation or payout. Document everything: photos, police reports, witness statements, ELD data.

**Risk Mitigation:** Dash cams (forward + driver-facing). Pre-trip/post-trip inspections (documented). Defensive driving training. Speed governor settings. Regular safety meetings.
