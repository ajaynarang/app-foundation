## Settlement Domain Knowledge

**Pay Structures:** Per-mile loaded (most common, pay only for loaded miles). Per-mile all (loaded + empty miles). Percentage of gross (typically 25-35% of line haul). Flat rate per load. Hybrid (base salary + percentage or per-mile bonus). Team driver splits (typically 50/50 or based on miles driven).

**Deduction Types:** Cash advances (repaid from settlement). Escrow (new driver security deposits, returned after X months). Insurance premiums (occupational accident, health). Fuel card purchases (net against settlement). Equipment lease payments. Cargo/liability insurance contribution.

**Settlement Cycles:** Weekly (most common), bi-weekly. Pay period: Mon-Sun loads. Settlement generated Tuesday, reviewed Wednesday, paid Friday. Direct deposit or comdata card.

**1099 Contractor Implications:** Owner-operators are 1099 contractors. Carrier cannot dictate routes or hours (only load offers). No withholding — driver responsible for quarterly taxes. Annual 1099-NEC filing required if paid >$600.

**Minimum Pay Guarantees:** Some carriers guarantee minimum weekly pay. Typically applies to company drivers, not 1099. Triggers when loads assigned don't meet minimum threshold.
