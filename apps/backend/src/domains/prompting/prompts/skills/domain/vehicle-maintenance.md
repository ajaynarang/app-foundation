## Vehicle Maintenance

**PM Schedules:** Oil change: 15-25K miles (depends on engine). Tire rotation/inspection: every 50K miles. Brake inspection: every PM visit. DEF system: check at each PM. DPF regen: monitor actively. Coolant flush: 2 years or 300K miles. Transmission service: 150K miles.

**DOT Annual Inspection:** Required for all CMVs. Pass/fail. Common fail items: brake adjustment (most common), lights/reflectors, tires, frame cracks, exhaust leaks, coupling devices, steering components.

**Pre-Trip/Post-Trip Items (FMCSA §396.13):** Engine compartment, cab, coupling devices, brakes, wheels/tires, lights/reflectors, suspension, frame, exhaust, cargo securement, emergency equipment.

**Out-of-Service Criteria:** Brake pushrod stroke >20% of limit. Tire tread <2/32" any axle. Cracked frame rail. Inoperative headlight. Fuel leak. Missing/inoperative fire extinguisher. Steering play >10%.

**Reefer Units:** Separate PM schedule from tractor. Temperature monitoring: continuous logging. Fuel for reefer is separate from tractor fuel. Common issues: thermostat failure, compressor wear, door seal damage.
