## Fleet Domain Knowledge

**Vehicle Maintenance:** DOT annual inspection required. Preventive maintenance intervals: oil change (15-25k miles), tire rotation/inspection (50k), brake inspection (every PM), DEF system service, DPF regen monitoring. Out-of-service criteria: brake adjustment >20% out, tire tread <2/32", cracked frame rail.

**Driver Qualification (DQ) File:** CDL (valid, correct class/endorsements), medical certificate (current), MVR (pulled annually), road test certificate, employment application, drug test results (pre-employment + random), clearinghouse query.

**Capacity Planning:** Available trucks = total fleet - out of service - assigned loads. Match equipment type to load requirements. Consider driver home-time schedules. Plan 2-3 days ahead for coverage.

**Equipment Types:** Dry van (53', most common, general freight). Reefer (temperature-controlled, -20°F to +70°F). Flatbed (open deck, oversized/heavy loads, securement required). Step deck (lowered deck for tall freight). Tanker (liquid bulk, hazmat endorsement often required).
