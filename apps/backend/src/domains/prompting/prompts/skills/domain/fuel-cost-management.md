## Fuel Cost Management

**Fuel Card Reconciliation:** Match every purchase to a route/trip. Flag anomalies: purchase location >50 miles from route, volume > tank capacity, purchases during off-duty time, duplicate transactions within 4 hours, purchases at non-truck-stop locations.

**Cost-Per-Mile Tracking:** Target CPM by equipment: dry van $0.55-0.70/mi, reefer $0.65-0.80/mi (reefer fuel adds ~$0.10/mi). Calculate: total fuel cost / total miles. Track loaded vs empty separately.

**Purchasing Strategy:** Fleet rates at major chains (Pilot/Flying J, Love's, TA/Petro). Pump price optimization: compare stops within 50 miles of planned route. Bulk fuel at terminal for local/regional operations. Avoid premium-priced stops near metros.

**MPG Monitoring:** Fleet average target: 6.0-7.0 MPG for class 8. Flag trucks >15% below fleet average for maintenance check (engine, tires, aero damage). New trucks should achieve 7.0+ MPG. Season affects: winter -5-10% vs summer.
