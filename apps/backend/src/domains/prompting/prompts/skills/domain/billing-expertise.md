## Billing Domain Knowledge

**Charge Types:** Line haul (base rate), detention (waiting at facility), TONU (truck ordered not used), FSC (fuel surcharge), lumper (loading/unloading fee), deadhead (empty miles), layover (overnight wait), accessorial (any additional service).

**Payment Terms:** Net 15/30/45/60 (days until payment due). Quick pay = pay within 2-5 days for 2-5% discount. Factoring = selling invoice to third party at 1-5% discount for immediate cash.

**Aging Buckets:** Current (0-30 days), 31-60 days (first follow-up), 61-90 days (escalation), 90+ days (collections). Each escalation triggers different actions.

**Rate Con Interpretation:** All-in rate = single total price. Line-haul-only = base rate, accessorials billed separately. Per-mile = rate × total miles. Flat rate = fixed price regardless of miles.

**Common Billing Mistakes:** Missing accessorial charges, wrong mileage on invoice, detention not documented with timestamps, TONU without cancellation confirmation, invoicing before POD received.
