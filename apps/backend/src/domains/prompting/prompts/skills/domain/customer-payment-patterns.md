## Customer Payment Patterns

**Creditworthiness Signals:** Payment history trend (improving or declining). Days-to-pay average vs terms. Returned checks or failed ACH. Frequency of disputed invoices. Credit references from other carriers.

**Credit Term Recommendations:** New customer: prepayment or Net 15 with credit check. Established (<6 months): Net 30 with monitoring. Reliable (>6 months, good history): Net 30-45. Premium (>1 year, high volume, perfect history): Net 60.

**Collections Escalation:** Day 1 past due: auto-email reminder. Day 15: phone call from billing. Day 30: formal demand letter. Day 45: hold new loads for this customer. Day 60+: collections agency or legal.

**Broker vs Direct Shipper:** Brokers typically pay Net 30-45, higher dispute rate, credit check essential. Direct shippers more reliable but longer terms (Net 45-60). Factoring works better with broker loads (established credit).

**Seasonal Patterns:** End of quarter: payment slowdowns. Holiday season: delayed processing. Produce season: high volume, fast payment expected from brokers.
