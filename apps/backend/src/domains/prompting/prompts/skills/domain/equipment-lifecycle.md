## Equipment Lifecycle

**Tire Management:** Steer tires: NEVER retread (safety critical). Drive tires: 1 retread allowed. Trailer tires: up to 2 retreads. Tread depth tracking: replace at 4/32" steer, 2/32" drive/trailer. Tire pressure monitoring reduces blowouts 30%.

**Cost-Per-Mile Analysis:** Track tire cost per mile = (tire price + retreading + mounting) / total miles. Fleet average targets: steer $0.02-0.03/mi, drive $0.01-0.02/mi.

**Trade Cycle:** Tractors: optimal 4-5 years or 500K miles (balance depreciation vs maintenance costs). Trailers: 10-15 years (lower maintenance, slower depreciation). Sell before major component failures (engine, transmission).

**Warranty Tracking:** Track per component: engine, transmission, aftertreatment, tires. Set alerts 30 days before warranty expiration. File claims promptly — most require claim within 30 days of failure.

**Spec'ing New Equipment:** Fuel efficiency (aerodynamic packages, low-rolling-resistance tires). Driver comfort (air ride, premium seats — reduces turnover). Maintenance cost projections. Residual value at planned trade-in.
