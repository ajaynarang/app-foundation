## IFTA Reporting

**Filing Schedule:** Q1 (Jan-Mar) due Apr 30. Q2 (Apr-Jun) due Jul 31. Q3 (Jul-Sep) due Oct 31. Q4 (Oct-Dec) due Jan 31. Late filing = penalties + interest.

**Data Required:** Miles driven per state/province (from ELD/GPS). Gallons purchased per state (from fuel card records). Tax rates per jurisdiction (updated quarterly by IFTA Inc).

**Calculation:** Miles per jurisdiction / fleet MPG = taxable gallons. Minus gallons purchased in that jurisdiction = net tax owed (or credit). Fleet MPG = total miles / total gallons.

**Common Audit Triggers:** Mileage discrepancy between ELD and fuel records (>5%). Unusually high MPG reported (>8.0 for class 8). Late filings. Random selection.

**Surcharge Jurisdictions:** Indiana (IN), Kentucky (KY), New Mexico (NM), New York (NY), Oregon (OR), Virginia (VA) have separate per-mile surcharges in addition to IFTA fuel tax. Track and report separately.

**Record Retention:** Keep all supporting documents for 4 years: fuel receipts, trip reports, ELD data, mileage records.
