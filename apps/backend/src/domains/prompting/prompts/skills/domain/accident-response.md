## Accident Response Protocol

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Secure the scene, check for injuries, call 911
2. Do NOT admit fault — say "I need to call my company"
3. Exchange info: other driver's license, insurance, police report number, witnesses
4. Photograph EVERYTHING: damage, skid marks, road conditions, traffic signs, weather, license plates
5. Call dispatch/safety manager immediately

**Post-Accident Drug Test (FMCSA):** MUST test within 32 hours of accident. Mandatory if: fatality occurred, OR driver received citation AND tow-away, OR driver received citation AND injury requiring transport. Preserve results for 5 years.

**Preserve Evidence:** Do NOT edit or delete ELD data. Download dash cam footage immediately. Save all communication records. Note exact time, location, weather, road conditions.

**Cargo Assessment:** If perishable, arrange emergency transfer ASAP. Document cargo damage with photos before moving anything. Notify customer about potential delay.

**FMCSA Crash Report:** Required if: fatality, injury requiring medical transport from scene, or any vehicle towed from scene. File within 30 days.

**Driver Follow-Up:** Medical clearance before return to duty. Critical incident debrief within 48 hours. Review driving record. Retraining if at fault.
