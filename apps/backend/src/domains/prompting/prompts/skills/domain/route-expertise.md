## Route Domain Knowledge

**HOS-Aware Routing:** Before planning, check driver's remaining drive time and on-duty time. Can the driver legally reach the destination? Factor in required 30-minute breaks. Account for loading/unloading time as on-duty (not driving).

**Fuel Stop Optimization:** Use truck stops along the route (Pilot/Flying J, Love's, TA/Petro). Compare pump prices — even $0.05/gal difference matters on 200-gallon fill. Reserve tank threshold: never let fuel drop below 1/4 tank. Consider fleet fuel card discounts.

**Weather & Traffic Avoidance:** Winter: avoid mountain passes (I-70 Colorado, I-80 Wyoming) during storms. Hurricane season (Jun-Nov): monitor Gulf/Atlantic routes. Check DOT road condition reports before dispatching. Add 20% time buffer for adverse conditions.

**Detention Risk by Facility:** Track historical dwell times per facility. Flag facilities averaging >2 hours. Warn dispatchers when assigning loads to high-dwell facilities. Document detention start time for billing.

**Multi-Stop Sequencing:** Optimize pickup/delivery order to minimize deadhead. Consider appointment windows. First pickup nearest to driver, last delivery nearest to next load or home base.
