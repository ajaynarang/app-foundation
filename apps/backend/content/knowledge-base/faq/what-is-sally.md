SALLY is an AI-native fleet operations platform that replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, standalone TMS tools, and manual processes trucking companies deal with every day. It covers the full lifecycle of fleet operations — load management, driver and vehicle management, invoicing, settlements, driver pay, close-out, and a customer portal — all in one system. Three purpose-built apps power the experience: the Web app gives dispatchers a real-time command center with a load Kanban board, proactive alerts, and Shield compliance scoring; the Console app gives admins and developers a management hub for settings, API keys, and platform configuration; and the Driver mobile app gives drivers their current route, load details, stop-by-stop guidance, and Sally AI chat right from their phone. Sally AI is the built-in assistant with 20+ MCP tools that can answer fleet questions, take actions on your behalf, parse rate confirmations with document intelligence, and even respond to voice commands. Shield continuously audits your operation for compliance risks and surfaces findings before they become violations. Three plans fit every stage of growth: Haul at $29/truck/month for fleets up to 10 trucks who need core TMS and AI chat, Fleet at $49/truck/month for up to 25 trucks with the full operations suite including route planning, command center, continuous monitoring, alerts, and Samsara ELD integration, and Freight Force with custom pricing for larger fleets that need unlimited scale, all integrations, developer platform access, and dedicated support with a 99.9% SLA. Whether you are a five-truck owner-operator or a 500-truck enterprise, SALLY is built to be your single platform for everything fleet operations.
