SALLY is built to scale with your fleet. The Haul plan at $29/truck/month supports fleets of up to 10 trucks and 5 users — it gives you the complete core TMS including loads, fleet management, billing, driver pay, close-out, a driver app, a customer portal, and Sally AI chat, which is everything a smaller operation needs to get off spreadsheets and run professionally. The Fleet plan at $49/truck/month is our most popular tier, supporting up to 25 trucks and 25 users — it unlocks the full operations suite with route planning, command center, continuous monitoring across 14 trigger types, proactive alerts, Shield compliance, Sally AI with actions and voice and document intelligence, and Samsara ELD integration. For fleets that have outgrown 25 trucks or need advanced integrations and developer tools, Freight Force offers custom pricing with unlimited trucks and users, all integrations including QuickBooks and custom TMS connectors, API keys, webhooks, OAuth, and priority support with a dedicated customer success manager. Every plan includes the same enterprise-grade security, multi-tenant data isolation, and the core TMS foundation — the difference is operational capabilities, integration depth, and support level.
