Traditional TMS systems handle load booking and basic dispatch but leave route planning to drivers or static mapping tools, require separate software for billing and accounting, and offer no real-time monitoring or AI assistance. SALLY is a complete fleet operations platform that replaces the entire stack — load management, driver and vehicle management, invoicing, settlements, driver pay, close-out, customer portal, route optimization, command center, continuous monitoring, proactive alerts, Shield compliance, and an AI assistant — all in one system. The AI layer is what sets SALLY apart: Sally AI can parse rate confirmations with document intelligence, answer natural language questions about your fleet, take actions on your behalf, and respond to voice commands. The route planning engine builds HOS-compliant routes with automatic rest and fuel stop insertion, and the monitoring system watches those routes 24/7 and re-plans when conditions change. For companies that want to keep their existing TMS like McLeod or TMW during a transition, SALLY can integrate with it on the Freight Force plan so you can migrate at your own pace. But most customers find that SALLY's built-in TMS capabilities — available on every plan starting at $29/truck/month — make the standalone TMS redundant. SALLY is not an add-on to your current tools; it is a complete replacement designed for how modern fleets actually operate.
