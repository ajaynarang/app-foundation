Yes, SALLY includes a driver mobile app on every plan starting at Haul ($29/truck/month). Drivers can see their current load and route, view next stop details with addresses and appointment times, check HOS remaining time, report delays, confirm arrivals, and chat with Sally AI for quick answers — all through a simple interface designed for safe use on the road with large touch targets and concise information. On the Fleet plan ($49/truck/month) and above, the mobile experience gains Sally AI voice mode so drivers can interact hands-free while driving, plus the route displayed in the app reflects the full optimized plan with rest stops, fuel stops, and live ETAs from the route planning engine. Sally AI on Fleet also supports actions and document intelligence, so a driver can snap a photo of a rate confirmation and have Sally parse it automatically. Dispatchers can also access the web dashboard from tablets or smaller screens — the interface is responsive across devices. The driver app is not a stripped-down afterthought; it is a purpose-built experience that gives drivers everything they need without overwhelming them, and it keeps dispatchers informed with real-time status updates as drivers move through their routes.
