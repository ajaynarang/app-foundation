SALLY handles driver breaks at two levels depending on your plan. On every plan including Haul ($29/truck/month), the driver mobile app tracks HOS status so drivers can see when their 30-minute break is due (after 8 hours of driving) and how much time remains before their mandatory 10-hour rest period. On the Fleet plan ($49/truck/month) and above, the route planning engine takes this much further — it automatically calculates when each break and rest period will be needed along the route, then finds optimal rest locations considering available truck parking, amenities, and proximity to the route. If dock time at a pickup or delivery provides enough duration to satisfy the 30-minute break requirement, SALLY recognizes that and avoids adding an unnecessary stop, saving time without breaking any rules. The route planner also accounts for the 70-hour 8-day rolling cycle, so it catches situations where a driver is approaching their weekly limit even if daily hours look fine. Dispatchers on the Fleet plan can see HOS hours remaining for every driver in the command center, and the system proactively alerts before any driver gets close to a violation.
