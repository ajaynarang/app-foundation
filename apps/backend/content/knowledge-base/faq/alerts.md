SALLY's alert and continuous monitoring system is available on the Fleet plan ($49/truck/month) and above. The platform monitors 14 trigger types across categories including HOS approaching limits, driver not moving, dock delays exceeding thresholds, weather disruptions, and traffic-based ETA changes. Dispatchers receive proactive alerts in the command center before problems become violations or missed deliveries. Each alert carries a priority level, recommended actions, and can be acknowledged or resolved directly from the dashboard. Unacknowledged critical alerts automatically escalate after a configurable timeout so nothing falls through the cracks. The alert system works hand-in-hand with the route planning engine — when a trigger fires, SALLY can automatically re-plan the affected route and notify both the dispatcher and the driver. Shield compliance ties into alerts too, surfacing compliance risk findings alongside operational alerts so dispatchers have a single view of everything that needs attention. The Haul plan does not include the alerts or monitoring system, keeping the experience focused on core TMS operations for smaller fleets that manage dispatch manually.
