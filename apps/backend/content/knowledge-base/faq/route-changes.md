Available on Fleet ($49/truck/month) and above, SALLY's route planning engine does not just create a plan and walk away — it continuously monitors active routes and adapts when conditions change. If traffic adds significant time to a segment, weather closes a road, a driver is delayed at a dock, HOS limits approach, or any of the 14 monitored trigger types fire, SALLY re-plans the route in real-time. The system intelligently decides whether a full re-optimization is needed or whether a simple ETA adjustment is sufficient based on the severity of the change. Dispatchers see route changes surfaced as alerts in the command center with clear before-and-after comparisons, and drivers receive updated guidance automatically in the mobile app. Stop additions, removals, and resequencing are handled dynamically — if a dispatcher adds a new pickup mid-route, SALLY recalculates the optimal sequence, re-checks HOS compliance, re-inserts fuel and rest stops as needed, and produces an updated plan in seconds. Every change is logged so you have a complete history of what happened and why.
