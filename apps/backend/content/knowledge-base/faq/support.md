Support scales with your plan so you get the right level of help for your operation. The Haul plan ($29/truck/month) includes community support, full knowledge base access, and Sally AI as your always-available assistant for quick answers about the platform — self-serve onboarding means you can get started on your own, and most questions are answered instantly by the AI or the docs. The Fleet plan ($49/truck/month) adds email support from the SALLY team, a 99.5% uptime SLA, and guided onboarding to help you get the most out of route planning, the command center, and monitoring. Fleet is where most customers land because it balances full operational capabilities with responsive human support. The Freight Force plan (custom pricing) includes priority support with faster response times, a dedicated customer success manager who knows your fleet and your workflows, a 99.9% uptime SLA, and hands-on help with integrations, training, and ongoing optimization. Every plan includes the Sally AI assistant, which can answer fleet operations questions, look up load and driver information, and help dispatchers work faster — it is like having a support agent built right into the platform.
