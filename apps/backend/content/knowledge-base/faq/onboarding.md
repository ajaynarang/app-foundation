Onboarding scales with your plan. Haul ($29/truck/month) is self-serve — sign up, walk through the built-in Setup Hub that guides you through adding drivers, vehicles, and customers, configure your billing preferences, and start managing loads. Most Haul customers are operational within a day or two. The Fleet plan ($49/truck/month) includes guided onboarding where our team helps you connect your Samsara ELD integration, configure alert thresholds, set up route planning preferences, and train your dispatchers on the command center and monitoring workflows. Fleet onboarding typically takes one to two weeks depending on fleet size and integration complexity. Freight Force customers get dedicated onboarding with a customer success manager who handles everything from integration setup (QuickBooks, TMS connectors, custom integrations) to dispatcher and driver training sessions, data migration, and go-live support. Every new account starts with a 30-day free trial that includes full Professional-tier (Fleet) features, so you can experience route planning, the command center, alerts, Shield compliance, and Sally AI with actions and voice before you commit to a plan.
