SALLY integrates with Samsara ELD devices, available on the Fleet plan ($49/truck/month) and above. The integration pulls real-time driving status, remaining HOS hours, duty cycle information, GPS location, and vehicle telematics so that route plans always reflect the driver's actual available hours rather than estimates. When continuous monitoring is active, live ELD data feeds directly into the alert system — if a driver's HOS hours are approaching a limit, SALLY flags it before it becomes a violation and can automatically re-plan the route to insert a required rest stop. GPS tracking from the ELD integration also powers the command center's real-time fleet map, giving dispatchers a live view of where every truck is at any moment. On the Haul plan, dispatchers can still track HOS data manually within SALLY and drivers see their status in the mobile app — the ELD integration simply automates that data flow and makes it real-time. Freight Force customers who need additional telematics integrations or custom ELD connections can work with our team to build those through the developer platform.
