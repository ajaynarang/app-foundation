Hours of Service (HOS) regulations are FMCSA rules that limit how long truck drivers can drive and work — the 11-hour driving limit within a 14-hour on-duty window, a mandatory 30-minute break after 8 consecutive hours of driving, a 10-hour off-duty rest period between shifts, and the 70-hour rolling 8-day cycle cap. SALLY handles HOS at two levels. On every plan, the driver mobile app tracks HOS status so drivers and dispatchers can see remaining drive time, duty hours, and cycle hours at a glance. On the Fleet plan ($49/truck/month) and above, SALLY's route planning engine simulates every route segment against these FMCSA rules during planning, automatically inserting rest stops where the driver will need them and recalculating ETAs to reflect mandatory breaks. When paired with a Samsara ELD integration (also available on Fleet and above), routes always reflect the driver's actual real-time HOS data rather than estimates. Every HOS decision is logged with audit-ready reasoning so you have compliance documentation if you ever need it. The result is routes that are legal before the wheels start turning, and continuous monitoring that catches approaching violations before they happen.
