Yes, billing and invoicing are part of SALLY's core TMS and included on every plan starting at Haul ($29/truck/month). Generate invoices directly from delivered loads — SALLY auto-calculates linehaul, fuel surcharges, accessorial charges, and detention if dock time exceeds the estimated window. Send invoices to customers, track their status through draft, sent, paid, and overdue stages, record partial or full payments, and view AR aging reports with current, 30, 60, and 90+ day buckets. The settlements module handles driver pay with configurable pay structures, deductions, and line items so you can reconcile and close out pay periods cleanly. Invoice factoring is supported for carriers who need faster cash flow. The close-out workflow ties everything together — match loads to invoices to settlements and confirm that the books balance before closing a period. For companies that also use QuickBooks, SALLY syncs invoices and settlements bidirectionally so your accounting stays in lockstep, though QuickBooks integration is available on the Freight Force plan. Every plan gives you the full billing and payments toolkit out of the box.
