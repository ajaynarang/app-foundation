Every plan includes the dispatcher dashboard with a visual Kanban board for load management — create loads, assign drivers and vehicles, track status from draft through delivered, and manage the full dispatch workflow. Dispatchers can also chat with Sally AI to query fleet data using natural language, like "show me drivers near Chicago" or "which loads are overdue." On the Fleet plan ($49/truck/month) and above, the dashboard expands into a full command center with a real-time fleet map showing active routes with progress bars and ETA status, priority-sorted alerts from 14 monitored trigger types, fleet KPIs including on-time percentage and HOS status, and Shield compliance scores. The command center is where continuous monitoring comes to life — dispatchers see alerts surface in real time, acknowledge or resolve them without leaving the dashboard, and watch routes re-plan automatically when conditions change. Route planning is one click from the load detail view, and the plan appears instantly with optimized stop sequence, HOS-compliant rest stops, fuel stops, and ETAs for every waypoint. The Haul plan keeps things focused on the Kanban board and core dispatch workflow, which is all a smaller fleet needs to run efficiently.
