Yes, SALLY provides reporting and analytics as part of the core TMS on every plan. The billing module includes AR aging reports with current, 30, 60, and 90+ day buckets so you always know where your receivables stand. The settlements module tracks driver pay over time with detailed line items and deductions. Load profitability reporting shows revenue versus cost per load so you can identify your most and least profitable lanes and customers. The close-out reconciliation workflow helps you verify that loads, invoices, and settlements all balance before closing a period. On the Fleet plan ($49/truck/month) and above, the command center adds real-time operational KPIs including on-time delivery percentage, active HOS status across your fleet, alert patterns and resolution times, and fleet utilization metrics. Dispatchers can identify trends — which drivers consistently deliver on time, which routes generate the most alerts, which customers have the longest dock times — and use that data to make better dispatch decisions. Sally AI can also answer analytics questions in natural language, like "what was our on-time percentage last week" or "which loads had the highest fuel cost."
