Yes, SALLY includes a customer portal on every plan starting at Haul ($29/truck/month). Your shippers and brokers get self-service access where they can view active shipments, track deliveries in real time, and review delivery history. Each load generates a public tracking link that shows real-time status and a timeline of events — customers can share this with their own stakeholders without needing a SALLY login. From the dispatcher side, you manage customers directly within SALLY: invite them to the portal, set up their contacts for operations, billing, and claims, configure payment terms and credit limits, and track payment history. Customer management is part of the core TMS, so there is no extra cost or add-on required. The portal gives your customers visibility into their shipments without adding work for your dispatch team — fewer "where's my truck" phone calls and more self-service transparency.
