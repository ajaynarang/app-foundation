If you are running 1 to 10 trucks, you are probably doing everything yourself or with a very small team — dispatching loads, tracking deliveries by text message, invoicing customers from a template, calculating driver pay in a spreadsheet, and keeping compliance documents in a filing cabinet or a shared drive. It works, but it is slow, error-prone, and it does not scale. SALLY's Haul plan at $29 per truck per month is built for exactly this situation: a complete digital operations platform that replaces the disconnected tools without requiring an enterprise budget or an IT department to set up.

A typical day on Haul looks like this. You log into SALLY and see your loads on the Kanban board — what is in draft, what is pending, what is assigned, what is in transit, and what has been delivered. You create a new load with pickup and delivery details, assign your driver and truck, and the driver sees the assignment immediately on their mobile dashboard with stop details and time windows. As the driver makes progress, they confirm arrivals and report any delays with a tap. Your customer follows along on a real-time tracking link you shared — no phone calls asking "where is my truck." When the load delivers, you generate an invoice with one click and SALLY creates the line items from the load record, including auto-calculated detention if the driver waited longer than expected. At the end of the pay period, you run settlements and SALLY calculates what each driver earned based on the pay structure you configured — per mile, percentage, flat rate, or a hybrid. The settlement shows every load, every mile, every deduction. Approve it and you are done.

The ROI math is straightforward. At $29 per truck per month, a five-truck fleet pays $145 per month for SALLY. That replaces a standalone invoicing tool (typically $30 to $50/month), eliminates hours of weekly spreadsheet work on settlements and load tracking, reduces billing errors that delay payments, and gives your customers a professional portal experience that builds confidence and retention. Sally AI chat is included, so you can ask "show me unpaid invoices" or "what loads delivered this week" and get instant answers instead of running reports. For a small fleet, the time saved on invoicing and settlements alone typically covers the cost of the platform within the first month. And when you are ready to grow past 10 trucks and add route planning, monitoring, and compliance tools, the upgrade to Fleet is seamless — your data, drivers, customers, and history all carry forward.
