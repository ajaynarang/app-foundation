Most small and mid-size fleets run on a patchwork of disconnected tools: spreadsheets for load tracking, a basic TMS or load board for booking, standalone software for invoicing, a separate system for driver pay, phone calls and texts for driver check-ins, and manual HOS log reviews that happen after the fact rather than before a violation occurs. This setup works until it does not — and the failure modes are expensive. Suboptimal routes add 5 to 15 percent more miles than necessary. HOS violations that slip through manual reviews cost $16,000 per incident. Billing delays stretch receivables cycles. Double data entry across systems wastes hours every week and introduces errors that cause disputes.

SALLY replaces this patchwork at every level, and the right plan depends on where your operation sits today. On Haul ($29/truck/month, up to 10 trucks), you get the core TMS that eliminates spreadsheets and standalone billing tools entirely. Load management moves to a visual Kanban board with full lifecycle tracking. Invoicing auto-generates from delivered loads with detention calculation built in. Driver pay and settlements replace the spreadsheet formulas with configurable pay structures and an approval workflow. The customer portal gives your shippers real-time tracking links and self-service access. Sally AI chat lets dispatchers and drivers ask questions in plain language instead of digging through screens. For a fleet that is currently running on spreadsheets, Haul alone is a fundamental upgrade in how the business operates day to day.

On Fleet ($49/truck/month, up to 25 trucks), SALLY adds the operational intelligence layer that transforms dispatch from reactive to proactive. AI-powered route planning replaces the 45-minute manual process with a few clicks and produces better routes than even experienced dispatchers can build by hand. HOS simulation catches violations during planning rather than after the fact. Continuous monitoring watches every active route around the clock and alerts dispatchers to problems before they become crises. The command center gives you a single dashboard for everything happening across the fleet. Shield compliance keeps your regulatory house in order. Samsara ELD integration brings real-time telematics data into the platform.

On Freight Force (custom pricing, unlimited trucks), SALLY becomes a full enterprise platform. QuickBooks integration eliminates accounting double entry. External TMS connectors let you run SALLY alongside McLeod, TMW, or other systems without replacing them. The developer platform opens up API access, webhooks, and MCP connectors for building custom integrations and automating workflows with AI tools. The result is a single platform where every piece of fleet operations data lives together, every process is connected, and every decision is informed by real-time intelligence rather than stale spreadsheets and gut instinct.
