SALLY provides role-based team management on every plan, starting at Haul ($29/truck/month) which supports up to 5 users. As your team grows, Fleet ($49/truck/month) supports up to 25 users, and Freight Force removes the limit entirely.

The platform uses five roles that map to how trucking companies actually operate. Owner gets full access to every feature plus billing and account management. Admin has access to all operational features plus team management — adding users, managing invitations, and configuring settings. Dispatcher is the primary operational role with access to loads, fleet management, billing, settlements, and the full operations suite (on Fleet and above). Driver access is scoped to the driver's own assigned loads, routes, HOS data, and the Sally AI assistant. Customer access is limited to the customer portal — their own shipments, tracking, invoices, and documents.

Invite team members by email and they receive a secure link to create their account with the correct role and permissions already assigned. No manual permission configuration or access-level tweaking required. Manage your entire team from one screen showing active staff, active drivers, and pending invitations, with the ability to deactivate users when they leave the company. For advanced team settings, integration configuration, and developer tools, the SALLY Console provides a dedicated administration interface. Every tenant on SALLY is completely isolated — your data, users, and configurations are never visible to other companies on the platform, and role boundaries are enforced at the API level so there is no way for a driver to accidentally access dispatcher functions or for one customer to see another customer's shipments.
