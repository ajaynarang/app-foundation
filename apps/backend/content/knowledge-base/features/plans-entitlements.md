SALLY offers three plans designed to match the needs of fleets at different stages, from a single owner-operator to a large enterprise running hundreds of trucks. Every plan includes the core TMS that replaces spreadsheets and disconnected tools, and higher tiers add operational intelligence, integrations, and developer capabilities.

Haul is the starter plan at $29 per truck per month, supporting up to 10 trucks and 5 users. It includes the full core TMS: load management with the visual Kanban board, driver and vehicle management, billing and invoicing with auto-detention calculation, driver pay and settlements with configurable pay structures, the customer portal with real-time tracking links, the driver mobile experience, and team management with role-based access. Sally AI is included in chat mode — drivers and dispatchers can ask questions and get answers about their loads, schedules, and fleet data. Haul does not include the operations suite (command center, route planning, continuous monitoring, alerts, or Shield compliance), integrations, or developer tools. It is the right starting point for small fleets that need to digitize their operations and get off spreadsheets. Community support and documentation are included.

Fleet is the professional plan at $49 per truck per month, supporting up to 25 trucks and 25 users, and it is the most popular choice for growing fleets. Fleet includes everything in Haul plus the full operations intelligence suite: AI-powered route planning with HOS simulation, continuous monitoring with all 14 trigger types, the command center dashboard, the dispatcher alert system with escalation rules, Shield compliance engine, and fuel optimization. Sally AI upgrades to full capability — actions (Sally can execute tasks, not just answer questions), voice mode for hands-free driver interaction, and document intelligence for rate-con parsing. Samsara ELD integration brings real-time telematics, HOS data, and vehicle diagnostics into SALLY. Email support and a 99.5% uptime SLA are included.

Freight Force is the enterprise plan with custom pricing based on fleet size and requirements, supporting unlimited trucks and users. It includes everything in Fleet plus the complete integration suite: Samsara, QuickBooks for accounting sync, external TMS connectors (McLeod, TMW, custom), and the developer platform with REST API access, API keys, webhooks, OAuth clients, and MCP connectors for AI assistants. Priority support with a dedicated customer success manager and a 99.9% uptime SLA round out the offering. For pricing on Freight Force, contact sally@appshore.in with your fleet size and requirements.
