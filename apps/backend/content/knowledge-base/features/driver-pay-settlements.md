SALLY handles driver pay from rate configuration to final settlement on every plan, starting at Haul ($29/truck/month). If you have been calculating driver pay in spreadsheets or manually pulling load data to figure out what each driver earned, this feature alone justifies the switch.

Set up pay structures per driver with the flexibility to match how your operation actually works: per-mile rates, percentage of load revenue, flat rate per load, or hybrid structures that combine a base rate with a percentage. When it is time to run settlements, select a date range and SALLY generates a complete settlement with load-by-load line items showing the revenue earned, miles driven, and calculated pay for each load. Add deductions for fuel advances, cash advances, insurance premiums, equipment leases, or escrow — whatever your operation requires. SALLY keeps a running balance so both you and your drivers can see exactly how pay was calculated.

Settlements follow an approval workflow — Draft, Approved, Paid — with audit trails showing who approved each settlement and when, which protects you in disputes and keeps your records clean for tax season. The close-out process ties settlements together with invoices to give you a complete financial picture for any period. On the Freight Force plan, settlement data syncs to QuickBooks automatically, so your accounting team sees finalized pay without re-entering anything. For fleets with multiple pay structures across different driver types, SALLY handles the complexity without requiring you to maintain separate spreadsheets or remember which driver gets which rate.
