SALLY includes a complete invoicing system on every plan, starting at Haul ($29/truck/month). Stop juggling standalone billing software or spreadsheet-based invoice tracking — SALLY handles the full accounts receivable cycle from invoice generation through payment collection, all connected to the loads and customers already in your system.

Generate invoices from delivered loads with a single click. SALLY automatically creates line items for linehaul charges, fuel surcharges, and accessorial charges based on the load record. The system even auto-calculates detention charges: if actual dock time exceeds the estimated window by more than two hours, it bills the excess at $75/hour with free time already deducted. Invoices follow a clear lifecycle — Draft, Sent, Paid, Partial, Overdue, or Void — with sequential numbering (INV-YYYY-####) so your records stay clean and auditable. Record multiple payments per invoice, track partial payments, and see exactly what is still owed at any point. The AR summary dashboard shows aging buckets (current, 1-30 days, 31-60 days, 61-90 days, 90+ days) so you always know your receivables position and can follow up on overdue accounts before they become collection problems.

Send invoices directly to customers from SALLY, and if you need to factor receivables, the system supports factoring workflows. For fleets that want their accounting system to stay in sync, QuickBooks integration is available on the Freight Force plan — invoices sync automatically so your bookkeeper or accountant sees every transaction without manual data entry. Whether you are a five-truck operation sending ten invoices a month or a fifty-truck fleet processing hundreds, SALLY's billing scales with you.
