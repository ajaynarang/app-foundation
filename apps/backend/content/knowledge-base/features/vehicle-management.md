SALLY gives you a complete vehicle inventory on every plan, starting at Haul ($29/truck/month). This is core TMS functionality for managing your rolling assets, tracking specs, and keeping your fleet organized as it grows.

Each vehicle record captures the details that matter for dispatch and compliance: make, model, year, VIN, license plate, equipment type (dry van, flatbed, reefer, tanker, step deck, power only), fuel tank capacity, miles per gallon, and gross vehicle weight. Vehicle status tracking (available, assigned, in shop, out of service) gives dispatchers instant visibility into what equipment is ready for the next load. Sleeper berth configuration is recorded per vehicle, which becomes important for HOS planning on longer routes.

On Fleet ($49/truck/month) and above, vehicle data feeds directly into the route planning engine. SALLY uses each vehicle's actual tank capacity and fuel consumption rate to calculate range precisely and insert fuel stops at the cheapest stations along the route — no more guessing or padding fuel budgets with manual estimates. With the Samsara integration on Fleet plans, telematics data flows into vehicle records automatically, giving you real-time location, fuel level, and diagnostic information. If your vehicles sync from an external TMS (available on Freight Force), SALLY locks identity fields like unit number, VIN, and license plate to prevent sync conflicts while keeping operational fields fully editable by your dispatchers.
