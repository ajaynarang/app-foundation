SALLY's alert system turns monitoring data into actionable notifications that tell dispatchers exactly what happened and what to do about it. Available on Fleet ($49/truck/month) and Freight Force plans, the alert system is the bridge between SALLY's continuous monitoring engine and the humans making decisions in the dispatch office.

Every alert carries a severity level — critical, high, medium, or low — along with a human-readable description, a recommended action, and a direct link to the affected route, driver, or load. Alert types span the full range of fleet operations: HOS alerts warn when drivers approach driving or duty limits, safety alerts flag speeding or breakdown events, delay alerts surface dock delays and ETA drift, compliance alerts catch missed check-ins or document expirations, and operational alerts cover everything from low fuel to unauthorized stops. Dispatchers interact with alerts through a simple workflow: acknowledge an alert to signal you are handling it, add notes to document context or decisions, and resolve it when the issue is closed. This creates a complete audit trail of how your team responded to every operational event.

The system is designed to reduce noise rather than add to it. Multiple triggers from the same root cause — say a traffic jam that causes both an ETA delay and an HOS concern — consolidate into a single actionable alert rather than flooding the dispatcher with duplicates. Unacknowledged critical alerts escalate automatically after a configurable timeout, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks during shift changes. Alert thresholds are fully configurable per tenant through the SALLY Console, so you can tune sensitivity to match your operation's tolerance levels and priorities.
