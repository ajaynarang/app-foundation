SALLY provides complete driver lifecycle management on every plan, starting at Haul ($29/truck/month). This is core TMS functionality — you do not need an operations plan to manage your driver roster, track compliance documents, or onboard new team members.

Add drivers with their full professional profile: CDL number, endorsements, medical card expiration date, emergency contacts, and home terminal. Drivers move through a clear status lifecycle — Pending Activation, Active, and Inactive — with full audit trails for each transition so you always know who changed what and when. When you activate a driver, SALLY can send them a portal invitation in the same step, giving them immediate access to the driver mobile experience where they can view their assigned loads, track their route, and communicate with dispatch through Sally AI chat.

On Fleet ($49/truck/month) and above, driver management integrates with the operations suite. SALLY pulls real-time HOS data from connected Samsara ELD devices, displaying remaining driving hours, duty window status, and cycle hours directly on the driver profile. If you are not using ELD integration, dispatchers can manage HOS data manually. Each driver can set personal preferences for rest stop types, fuel stop preferences, and break reminders, and the route planning engine respects these preferences when building plans. If your fleet syncs drivers from an external TMS (available on Freight Force), SALLY protects synced identity fields like CDL numbers while letting dispatchers freely update operational details like preferences, contacts, and status.
