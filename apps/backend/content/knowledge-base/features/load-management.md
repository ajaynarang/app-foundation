Load management is the heart of SALLY's TMS, available on every plan starting at Haul ($29/truck/month). It replaces the spreadsheets, whiteboards, and disconnected tools that most small fleets use to track their freight, and it scales with you as your operation grows.

SALLY presents loads on a visual Kanban board organized by lifecycle stage: Draft, Pending, Assigned, In Transit, and Delivered. Drag loads between columns or let status transitions happen automatically as drivers report progress. Create loads with multiple pickup and delivery stops, specify time windows for each stop, set equipment requirements and commodity types, and record rates. Assign drivers and vehicles directly from the load detail panel, or let loads flow in automatically from a connected TMS (Freight Force plan) or from customer requests through the customer portal. Each load gets a unique tracking link that you can share with customers — they follow delivery progress in real-time without needing a login, and the page auto-refreshes every 60 seconds.

For recurring freight, duplicate any existing load to create a new one with the same stops, requirements, and rates pre-filled. Load numbers are auto-generated daily with a clean sequential format, and the system tracks where each load came from — whether it was created manually by a dispatcher, synced from an external TMS, or requested through the customer portal. On Fleet ($49/truck/month) and above, SALLY adds AI-powered rate confirmation parsing: upload a rate-con PDF and Sally's document intelligence extracts shipper details, stops, time windows, rates, and equipment requirements automatically, creating the load for you with minimal manual entry.
