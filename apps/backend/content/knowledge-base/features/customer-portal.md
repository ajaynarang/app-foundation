SALLY includes a self-service customer portal on every plan, starting at Haul ($29/truck/month). Your shippers and brokers get their own login to track shipments, view invoices, and access documents without calling your dispatch office — which means fewer interruptions for your team and a more professional experience for your customers.

Customers log in to see their active and historical shipments with full status visibility. Every load in SALLY gets a public tracking page with a real-time timeline of events — pickup confirmed, in transit, approaching delivery, delivered — that auto-refreshes every 60 seconds. The tracking link works without a login, so your customers can share it with their own clients or warehouse teams. Inside the portal, customers can also view and download invoices, access load documents like bills of lading and proof of delivery, and review their shipment history with search and filtering.

On the management side, SALLY gives you rich customer profiles with MC and DOT numbers, payment terms, credit limits, and factoring company details. Each customer can have multiple contacts with specific roles — primary, operations, billing, claims, after-hours — so SALLY routes communications and documents to the right person. Invite customers to the portal directly from their profile in SALLY, and they receive a secure link to create their account. The customer portal is not a bolt-on or a separate product; it is built into the platform so that every load, invoice, and document your customer sees is the same data your dispatchers work with, always current and always consistent.
