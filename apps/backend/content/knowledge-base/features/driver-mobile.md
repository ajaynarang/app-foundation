Every SALLY plan includes a mobile-optimized driver interface, starting at Haul ($29/truck/month). Drivers access SALLY through their phone or tablet browser — no separate app to install — and get a dashboard built specifically for life on the road: large touch targets, clear typography, and only the information that matters for the current moment.

The driver dashboard shows current status at a glance: assigned load details, next stop with ETA, HOS hours remaining, and any active alerts or messages from dispatch. Drivers view their route as a step-by-step timeline with completed stops, the current segment in progress, and upcoming stops ahead. As the route progresses, drivers can report delays with standardized reason codes, confirm arrivals at stops, and log fuel purchases. Every interaction is designed for quick taps rather than typing, because drivers need to get information and report status without taking their eyes off the road for long.

Sally AI chat is available on all plans, letting drivers ask plain-language questions like "what is my next stop" or "when do I need to take my break" and get concise, useful answers. On Fleet ($49/truck/month) and above, Sally AI adds voice mode — drivers speak their questions and hear responses read back, making the experience truly hands-free. Voice mode is designed for on-the-road safety: responses are short and direct, avoiding long explanations that would distract from driving. On Fleet plans, Sally AI can also take actions on the driver's behalf, like reporting a delay or requesting a route update, through natural conversation rather than navigating through menus.
