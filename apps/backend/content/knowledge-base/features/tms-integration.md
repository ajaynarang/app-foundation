SALLY is a complete TMS on its own, but for larger fleets that have already invested in systems like McLeod or TMW, the External TMS Integration feature lets you run SALLY alongside your existing platform without replacing it. Available exclusively on the Freight Force plan (custom pricing, unlimited trucks and users), this is designed for enterprises that want SALLY's AI-powered operations intelligence without ripping out the system of record their team already knows.

The integration works bidirectionally. Load assignments, driver profiles, vehicle data, and customer information sync from your TMS into SALLY, so dispatchers can use SALLY's route planning, monitoring, and command center against the same data that lives in your primary system. When SALLY updates a load's status — an ETA change from the monitoring engine, a delivery confirmation from a driver — those updates flow back to the TMS automatically. This means your accounting team, your customers, and any other downstream systems that read from the TMS stay in sync without anyone doing double data entry.

Smart sync protection is a key design principle. SALLY identifies which fields are managed by the external TMS — things like driver CDL numbers, vehicle VINs, and load reference numbers — and locks them to prevent overwrites. At the same time, operational fields that SALLY manages, like route plans, HOS calculations, and alert responses, remain fully editable by your dispatchers. The result is a clean division of responsibility: your TMS handles the commercial and administrative record-keeping, and SALLY handles the real-time operational intelligence. If your fleet uses a TMS not listed here, contact sally@appshore.in to discuss custom connectors — the Freight Force plan includes support for building adapters to proprietary systems.
