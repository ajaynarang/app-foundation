Shield is SALLY's proactive compliance engine, available on Fleet ($49/truck/month) and Freight Force plans. Instead of waiting for an FMCSA audit to discover problems, Shield continuously evaluates your fleet's compliance posture and surfaces issues before they become violations, fines, or threats to your operating authority.

Shield works by running automated audits across your fleet data — driver qualifications, vehicle inspections, HOS records, document status, and operational patterns. Each audit produces findings categorized by severity: critical issues that need immediate attention, warnings that should be addressed soon, and informational items that represent best-practice recommendations. Findings come with clear descriptions of what is wrong, why it matters from a regulatory perspective, and what specific action will resolve it. As your team addresses findings, Shield tracks remediation progress so you can demonstrate a pattern of proactive compliance management.

At the fleet level, Shield generates a compliance score that gives you an at-a-glance view of your operation's regulatory health. The score reflects the aggregate state of all active findings — a fleet with no open critical findings and only a handful of warnings will score high, while unresolved critical items drag the score down quickly. This scoring system makes it easy to set internal compliance targets and track improvement over time. Shield integrates directly with SALLY's alert system, so critical compliance findings generate dispatcher alerts with the same acknowledge-and-resolve workflow used for operational issues. Document compliance monitoring tracks CDL expirations, medical card renewals, insurance certificates, and other time-sensitive credentials, alerting you well before anything lapses. For fleets preparing for DOT audits or working to improve their CSA scores, Shield provides the continuous oversight that turns compliance from a periodic scramble into a managed, measurable process.
