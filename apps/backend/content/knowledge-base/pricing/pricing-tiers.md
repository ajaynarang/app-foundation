SALLY offers three pricing tiers — Haul, Fleet, and Freight Force — designed for fleet operators of every size. All plans start with a 30-day free trial with full Professional-level access. Annual billing saves 2 months (pay for 10, get 12).

HAUL (Starter): Up to 10 trucks at $29 per truck per month. Built for owner-operators and small carriers who need a modern TMS without the enterprise price tag. Includes the complete Core TMS suite: load management, fleet management (drivers, vehicles), billing and invoicing, driver pay and settlements, close-out and reconciliation, and the driver mobile app. Sally AI chat is included for quick answers and lookups. Support is community-based with full documentation access. Self-serve onboarding — sign up and start dispatching in minutes.

FLEET (Professional): Up to 25 trucks at $49 per truck per month. The most popular plan — built for growing fleets that need real-time visibility, AI-powered operations, and proactive compliance. Includes everything in Haul plus: Sally AI with actions, voice mode, and document intelligence (rate-con parsing); command center with live fleet map; AI-powered route planning with HOS compliance; continuous monitoring (14 trigger types, 24/7 surveillance); dispatcher alert system with escalation; Shield compliance engine with scoring and audits; and Samsara ELD integration. Email support with a 99.5% uptime SLA and guided onboarding.

FREIGHT FORCE (Enterprise): Unlimited trucks with custom pricing. Built for large carriers and enterprise logistics operations that need full platform access, custom integrations, and dedicated support. Includes everything in Fleet plus: all integrations (Samsara, QuickBooks, TMS connectors, custom integrations); full developer platform (API keys, webhooks, OAuth clients); priority support with a dedicated Customer Success Manager; 99.9% uptime SLA; and dedicated onboarding with custom training. Contact sally@appshore.in for a custom quote.

All plans include a 30-day free trial with full Professional-level features — no credit card required. After your trial, choose the plan that fits your fleet. Trial accounts transition to trial-expired status after 30 days; contact sales to activate a paid plan.

Key differences at a glance: Haul gives you core TMS + Sally AI chat. Fleet adds the full operations intelligence suite (command center, route planning, monitoring, alerts, Shield) plus Samsara integration. Freight Force unlocks all integrations, the developer platform, and enterprise-grade support.
