# Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in. What you see depends on your plan tier.

## Command Center (Fleet and Freight Force Plans)

> This feature requires the Fleet plan or higher.

The command center is a real-time operations dashboard designed for active fleet management. It includes:

- **Fleet map**: A live map showing all driver positions with color-coded status indicators (in transit, at stop, off duty). Click any driver to see their current load, ETA, and HOS status.
- **Active load count**: A summary of loads currently in transit, loads awaiting dispatch, and loads delivered today.
- **Alert summary**: Recent alerts requiring attention, grouped by priority. Click any alert to view details and take action.
- **Quick actions**: Assign loads, acknowledge alerts, and view driver details directly from the map.

See also: [Command Center](/docs/manual/web-app/dispatcher/command-center)

## Loads Kanban (Haul Plan)

Haul plan users land on the **Loads** page, which displays a Kanban board organizing loads by lifecycle stage: Draft, Booked, Dispatched, In Transit, and Delivered. Drag loads between columns to update status, or click any load card to open its detail sheet.

The Kanban board provides a clear visual overview of your operation's current state and is the primary workflow surface for smaller fleets.

## Sidebar Navigation

The sidebar on the left lists all available areas. The sections you see depend on your role and plan:

- **Loads**: Load board and Kanban view (all plans)
- **Fleet**: Drivers, Vehicles, Customers (all plans)
- **Billing**: Invoices and payment tracking (all plans)
- **Pay**: Settlements and driver pay (all plans)
- **Close Out**: Period-end reconciliation (all plans)
- **Plans**: Route planning (Fleet+)
- **Alerts**: Alert management (Fleet+)
- **Command Center**: Real-time operations dashboard (Fleet+)
- **Shield**: Compliance engine (Fleet+)

Items with a **sparkle icon** next to their name require a higher plan. Clicking them shows which plan is needed.
