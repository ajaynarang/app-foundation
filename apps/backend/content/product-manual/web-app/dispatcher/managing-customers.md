# Managing Customers

Customers are the shippers and brokers who book loads with your carrier. SALLY tracks customer information, load history, billing, and provides a self-service portal for your customers.

## Viewing Customers

Navigate to **Fleet → Customers** tab. The customer list shows company name, contact person, total loads, and outstanding invoice balance. Search by company name or contact name.

## Adding a Customer

1. Click **+ New Customer** in the top-right corner.
2. Fill in the customer details:
   - **Company name**: The customer's business name.
   - **Contact name**: Primary point of contact.
   - **Email**: Used for invoice delivery and portal access.
   - **Phone**: Contact phone number.
   - **Billing address**: Address for invoicing purposes.
3. Click **Create Customer**.

## Customer Detail View

Click any customer to open their detail sheet:

- **Load history**: View all loads booked by this customer, filterable by status and date range.
- **Payment history**: Track payments received, outstanding balances, and overdue invoices.
- **Outstanding invoices**: See all unpaid invoices with aging information.
- **Edit details**: Update contact information and billing address.

## Recurring Lanes

For customers with repeat shipments on the same routes, set up recurring lanes:

1. Open the customer detail sheet.
2. Navigate to the **Lanes** section.
3. Click **+ Add Lane** and define the origin, destination, rate, and frequency.
4. When creating a new load for this customer, recurring lanes appear as quick-fill options, reducing data entry.

## Customer Portal

Each customer can access a self-service portal with their own login credentials. Through the portal, customers can:

- View their shipments and current status
- Track deliveries in real-time via tracking links
- Download documents (BOL, POD, rate confirmation)
- View and download invoices

To grant portal access, ensure the customer record has a valid email address. Invite them from the customer detail sheet.
