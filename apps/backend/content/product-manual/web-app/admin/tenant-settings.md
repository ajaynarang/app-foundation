# Tenant Settings

Tenant settings control your organization's basic configuration in SALLY. These settings affect all users across your company.

## Accessing Settings

Navigate to **Settings → General** in the Web App sidebar. Admin and Owner roles have access to these settings.

## General Settings

- **Company name**: Your organization's name as displayed throughout SALLY, on invoices, and in the customer portal.
- **Address**: Company mailing address. Used on invoices and official documents.
- **Timezone**: Default timezone for your organization. Affects how times are displayed for loads, routes, alerts, and reports. Individual users can override their timezone in their profile.
- **Default currency**: The currency used for rates, invoices, and settlements (typically USD).

## Branding

- **Company logo**: Upload your logo to appear on invoices, the customer portal, and email communications. Supported formats: PNG, JPG, SVG. Recommended size: 200x200 pixels minimum.

## Saving Changes

After making changes, click **Save**. Changes take effect immediately for all users in your organization. Some changes (like logo updates) may take a moment to propagate to invoices and the customer portal.

## Advanced Settings

For more detailed organization configuration — including legal entity name, DOT number, MC number, and additional branding options — visit **Console → Account → Organization**.
