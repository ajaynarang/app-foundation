# Sally AI Assistant for Drivers

Sally AI is your on-the-road assistant, available on every screen of the driver app. Sally can answer questions about your route, HOS, loads, and pay — and on Fleet plans, respond to voice commands for hands-free operation.

## Opening Sally

Tap the **chat icon** in the bottom-right corner of any screen, or use the Sally shortcut from the home screen. The chat window opens over your current view.

## What You Can Ask

Sally understands natural language questions related to your driving operations:

- **Route questions**: "What's my next stop?", "How far is the delivery?", "When do I arrive at the pickup?"
- **HOS questions**: "How many hours do I have left?", "When is my next break?", "Can I make it to the delivery before my hours run out?"
- **Load questions**: "What am I hauling?", "What's the reference number for this load?", "Who is the customer?"
- **Pay questions**: "What's my settlement for this week?", "How many miles have I driven this period?"
- **Help questions**: "How do I report a delay?", "How do I update my arrival?"

## Reporting Updates

You can use Sally to communicate status updates to dispatch:

- **Report a delay**: "I'm stuck at the dock" or "There's a traffic delay on I-95." Sally logs the information and can notify dispatch.
- **Report arrival**: "I've arrived at the pickup." Sally updates your status and timestamps the arrival.

## Voice Mode

> This feature requires the Fleet plan or higher.

Tap the **microphone icon** in the Sally chat to activate voice mode. Speak naturally — Sally transcribes your speech and responds with voice. Voice mode is designed for hands-free use while driving. Say "stop listening" to deactivate voice mode.

Voice mode supports all the same questions and reporting capabilities as text chat.

## Conversation History

Your conversation with Sally is preserved during your session. You can scroll back to review previous answers. Starting a new session clears the conversation history.
