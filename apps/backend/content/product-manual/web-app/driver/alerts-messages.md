# Alerts and Messages for Drivers

Stay informed about your assignments, schedule changes, and urgent items through the alerts and messages system.

## Alerts

Navigate to **Alerts** from the home screen or sidebar. Alerts are notifications generated by the system or sent by dispatch:

- **New load assignment**: You have been assigned a new load. Tap to view the load details and route.
- **Route change**: Your route plan has been updated by dispatch. Review the new stop sequence and ETAs.
- **Schedule update**: A pickup or delivery time window has changed. Check the updated times.
- **HOS warning**: You are approaching an HOS limit. Plan your next rest stop. (Fleet plan with Samsara)
- **Weather or traffic advisory**: Conditions along your route require attention.

### Responding to Alerts

- **Acknowledge**: Tap **Acknowledge** to confirm you have seen the alert. This notifies dispatch that you are aware of the situation.
- **Report an issue**: If an alert requires action you cannot take (e.g., a schedule change that is not feasible), use Sally AI or the Messages section to communicate with dispatch.

## Messages

The **Messages** section shows communications from dispatch. Messages may include instructions for a specific stop, updated customer contacts, or general fleet announcements.

Read messages as they arrive. Important messages are flagged and generate push notifications.

## Push Notifications

SALLY sends push notifications for critical alerts and new messages. To receive push notifications:

1. When prompted by the app, allow notification permissions.
2. Ensure notifications are enabled in your device settings for the SALLY app.

Push notifications ensure you do not miss new load assignments, urgent schedule changes, or critical alerts even when the app is in the background.
