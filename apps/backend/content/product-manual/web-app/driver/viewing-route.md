# Viewing Your Route

The route page shows your assigned route plan with all stops, timing, and HOS information. It is your guide for the day's driving.

## Route Overview

Navigate to the **Route** section from the home screen or sidebar. The route page displays:

- **Stop list**: All stops in order — pickups, deliveries, rest stops, and fuel stops. Your current or next stop is highlighted.
- **Map view**: A map showing the full route with all stops marked. Your current position is indicated with a blue marker.
- **Current segment**: The segment you are currently driving is highlighted on the map and in the stop list.

## Stop Details

Each stop in the list shows:

- **Location**: Address and facility name.
- **Type**: Pickup, delivery, rest, or fuel.
- **Time window**: The earliest and latest arrival times. Arriving within this window keeps you on schedule.
- **Estimated arrival**: SALLY's projected arrival time based on your current position, speed, and remaining distance.
- **Status**: Upcoming, current, or completed.

Tap any stop to see additional details such as contact name, phone number, special instructions, and dock assignment (if provided by dispatch).

## HOS Status Bar

The HOS status bar at the top of the route page shows your current hours of service state:

- **Remaining drive time**: Hours left in your 11-hour driving window.
- **Remaining duty time**: Hours left in your 14-hour on-duty window.
- **Break status**: Time until your 30-minute break is required, or confirmation that your break has been taken.
- **Cycle hours**: Remaining hours in your 70-hour/8-day cycle.

This information updates in real-time when your ELD is connected via Samsara (Fleet plan).

## Navigating to the Next Stop

Tap **Navigate** on the current stop to open turn-by-turn directions. SALLY provides the route optimized during planning, accounting for truck-specific restrictions.
