# Driver Home Screen

The home screen is the first thing you see when you open the SALLY driver app. It gives you an at-a-glance view of your current assignment and quick access to everything you need on the road.

## Active Load View

When you have an active load assignment, the home screen displays:

- **Current load**: Load reference number, customer name, and load status.
- **Next stop**: The address, type (pickup or delivery), and time window for your next stop.
- **ETA**: Estimated arrival time at the next stop based on your current position and route.
- **Quick actions**: Buttons to update your status, report arrival, or report a delay.

If you have an active route plan, a route overview bar shows the total number of stops remaining and the estimated completion time.

## No Active Assignment

When you do not have a load assigned, the home screen displays a "No active assignment" state. You will receive a push notification when dispatch assigns you a new load. Check the **Recent Loads** section to review your recently completed loads.

## Navigation

- **Route**: View your full route plan with all stops. See [Viewing Your Route](/docs/manual/web-app/driver/viewing-route).
- **Messages**: View messages and instructions from dispatch.
- **Alerts**: See notifications about schedule changes, route updates, or urgent items.
- **Sally AI**: Tap the chat icon to ask Sally questions or report updates. See [Sally AI Assistant](/docs/manual/web-app/driver/sally-ai-assistant).

## Recent Loads

Scroll down on the home screen to see your recently completed loads with delivery dates, customers, and destinations. Tap any load to view its details.
