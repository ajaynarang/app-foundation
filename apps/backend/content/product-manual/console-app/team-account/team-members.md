# Team Members

Manage your organization's team from Console with full control over invitations, roles, and access.

## Accessing Team Members

Navigate to **Console → Team → Members**.

## Viewing Your Team

The members list shows every user in your organization with:

- **Name**: Full name as set in their profile.
- **Email**: Login email address.
- **Role**: Current assigned role (Dispatcher, Driver, Admin, Owner).
- **Status**: Active or Deactivated.
- **Last active**: The most recent date the user logged in or performed an action.

Search by name or email, or filter by role or status to find specific team members.

## Inviting a New Member

1. Click **Invite Member**.
2. Enter the user's **email address**.
3. Select the **role** to assign. See [Roles & Permissions](/docs/manual/reference/roles-permissions) for what each role can access.
4. Click **Send Invitation**.

The user receives an email with a link to create their account. Pending invitations appear in **Console → Team → Invitations**, where you can:

- **Resend**: Send the invitation email again if the user did not receive it.
- **Revoke**: Cancel the invitation before the user accepts it.

## Changing a Role

1. Click a team member to open their detail view.
2. Click **Edit Role**.
3. Select the new role and click **Save**.

Role changes take effect immediately. The user's available features and navigation update on their next page load.

## Deactivating a Member

1. Open the team member's detail view.
2. Click **Deactivate**.

Deactivated users cannot log in but their historical data remains intact. Deactivation frees up a slot against your plan's user limit. To restore access, click **Activate** from the same detail view.
