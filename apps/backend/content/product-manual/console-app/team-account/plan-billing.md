# Plan and Billing

View and manage your SALLY subscription from the plan and billing page.

## Accessing Plan & Billing

Navigate to **Console → Account → Plan & Billing**.

## Current Plan Overview

The plan overview displays:

- **Plan name**: Haul, Fleet, or Freight Force.
- **Fleet limit**: Maximum number of vehicles on your plan (10 for Haul, 25 for Fleet, unlimited for Freight Force).
- **Current vehicle count**: How many vehicles are currently in your fleet.
- **User limit**: Maximum team members (5 for Haul, 25 for Fleet, unlimited for Freight Force).
- **Current user count**: Active team members.

## Trial Status

If you are on a trial, the page shows:

- **Trial plan**: Which plan features you are evaluating (Fleet by default).
- **Days remaining**: Countdown to trial expiration.
- **Trial end date**: The exact date your trial expires.

The trial banner also appears at the top of the Web App as a reminder. Select a plan before the trial ends to avoid interruption.

## Feature Entitlements

A complete list of features shows what is enabled on your current plan. Each feature displays:

- **Feature name**: The capability (e.g., "Route Planning", "Sally AI Voice Mode").
- **Status**: Enabled (included in your plan) or Locked (requires a higher plan).
- **Required plan**: Which plan tier is needed to unlock a locked feature.

This list is the definitive reference for what your organization can access.

## Upgrading Your Plan

- **Haul to Fleet**: Click **Upgrade** and follow the prompts to move to the Fleet plan. Your fleet and user limits increase, and advanced features (command center, route planning, alerts, Shield, Samsara, Sally AI actions) become available.
- **Fleet to Freight Force**: Contact the SALLY sales team at sally@appshore.in for custom pricing and onboarding. Click **Contact Sales** on the plan page to initiate.

## Billing

- **Payment method**: Add or update your payment method (credit card, ACH).
- **Invoice history**: View past billing invoices from SALLY for your subscription.
