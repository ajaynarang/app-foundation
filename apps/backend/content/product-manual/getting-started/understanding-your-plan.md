# Understanding Your Plan

SALLY offers three plan tiers, each designed for different fleet sizes and operational needs. Your plan determines which features are available to your organization.

## Checking Your Current Plan

Navigate to **Console → Account → Plan & Billing** to view your current plan, fleet limit, active vehicle count, user limit, and a full list of enabled features.

## Trial Period

New organizations start with a **30-day trial** that includes all Fleet plan features. A trial countdown banner appears at the top of the screen showing days remaining. When the trial ends, your organization transitions to the plan you select — or defaults to Haul if no selection is made.

## Plan Tiers

### Haul — $29/month per truck (up to 10 trucks)

Core TMS capabilities for small fleets:
- Load management (create, assign, track, Kanban board)
- Fleet management (drivers, vehicles, customers)
- Billing and invoicing
- Driver pay and settlements
- Close-out and reconciliation
- Driver mobile app
- Sally AI chat (ask questions, look up data)

### Fleet — $49/month per truck (up to 25 trucks)

Everything in Haul, plus advanced operations:
- Sally AI actions, voice mode, and document intelligence
- Command center with live fleet map
- Route planning with HOS-compliant optimization
- Alerts and continuous monitoring (14 trigger types)
- Shield compliance engine and auditing
- Samsara ELD integration (GPS, HOS, telematics)

### Freight Force — Custom pricing (unlimited trucks)

Everything in Fleet, plus enterprise capabilities:
- All integrations (Samsara, QuickBooks, and future connectors)
- Developer platform (API keys, webhooks, OAuth clients)
- MCP server for connecting external AI assistants
- Priority support
- Custom onboarding and training

## Feature-Locked Indicators

In the Web App sidebar, features that require a higher plan display a **sparkle icon** next to their name. Clicking a locked feature shows an informational message about which plan is required.

## Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade, visit **Console → Account → Plan & Billing** and click **Upgrade**, or contact the SALLY sales team at sally@appshore.in for Freight Force pricing and custom arrangements.
