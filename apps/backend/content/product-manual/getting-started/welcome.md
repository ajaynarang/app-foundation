# Welcome to SALLY

SALLY is an AI-native fleet operations platform that brings dispatching, fleet management, billing, compliance, and AI assistance into a single system. Whether you manage five trucks or five hundred, SALLY provides the tools your team needs to move freight efficiently.

## Three Apps, One Platform

SALLY consists of three interconnected applications, each designed for a specific role in your operation:

**Web App** is where dispatchers and operations staff spend most of their time. It covers load management, fleet oversight, billing and invoicing, driver pay and settlements, compliance monitoring, route planning, and the command center. Dispatchers can manage the full lifecycle of a load from booking through delivery and close-out.

**Console** is the administration and configuration hub. Use Console to manage integrations (Samsara, QuickBooks), configure operational settings (HOS rules, alert thresholds, invoice templates), manage team members and roles, handle plan and billing, and access developer tools (API keys, webhooks, OAuth, MCP). Console opens in a separate tab with seamless single sign-on from the Web App.

**Driver App** is the mobile-optimized view for drivers on the road. Drivers see their current load, route with stop-by-stop details, ETA, HOS status, messages from dispatch, and have access to Sally AI for hands-free assistance.

## Navigating SALLY

The **sidebar** on the left is your primary navigation. It lists all available areas based on your role and plan. Items marked with a sparkle icon require a higher plan tier. The **header bar** at the top provides quick access to alerts, notifications, and your profile. The **Sally AI chat icon** (bottom-right) is available on every screen.

## Where to Find Help

- **Sally AI**: Click the chat icon or press **Cmd+K** to ask questions about your fleet data or how to use any feature.
- **Product Manual**: Sally can pull answers directly from this manual when you ask "how do I..." questions.
- **Support**: Reach the SALLY support team at support@appshore.in for issues that require human assistance.
