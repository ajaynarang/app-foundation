# First Login and Setup

This guide walks you through your first login and helps you get oriented in the SALLY platform.

## Logging In

1. Open the SALLY Web App URL provided by your organization.
2. Enter your email address and password. If your organization uses phone-based authentication, enter your phone number and the OTP code sent to you.
3. Click **Sign In**.

If you received an invitation email, click the link in that email to set your password before logging in.

## Completing Your Profile

1. After your first login, you may be prompted to complete your profile. Enter your **full name**, **phone number**, and **timezone**.
2. If you are a driver, you will also be asked for your CDL number and CDL expiry date.
3. Click **Save** to continue.

## Dashboard Orientation

Where you land after login depends on your role and plan:

- **Dispatchers on Fleet or Freight Force** land on the **Command Center** — a real-time operations dashboard with a fleet map, active loads, and alert summary.
- **Dispatchers on Haul** land on the **Loads** page — a Kanban board showing loads organized by lifecycle stage.
- **Drivers** land on the **Home** screen showing their current load assignment, next stop, and ETA.
- **Admins** land on the **Setup Hub** with guided steps to configure your organization (add drivers, connect integrations, set up billing).

## Key Navigation

- **Sidebar** (left): Lists all main areas — Loads, Fleet, Billing, Pay, Plans, Alerts, Command Center, Shield, Close Out, and more. Click any item to navigate. Items with a sparkle icon require a plan upgrade.
- **Header** (top): Shows notification bell for alerts, your profile menu, and organization selector.
- **Sally AI** (bottom-right): Chat icon available on every page. Click it or press **Cmd+K** to open.
